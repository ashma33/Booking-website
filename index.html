What We Offer

Everything you need for a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

Restaurant & Room Service

Enjoy Nepali and continental meals at our in-house restaurant, or order food straight to your room any time of day.

Swimming Pool

Take a dip in our clean outdoor pool, open every day for all guests staying at the hotel.

Spa & Massage

Relax with a traditional Nepali massage or a full spa treatment after a long day of sightseeing or travel.

Gym

Our gym is open 24 hours with basic cardio machines and free weights — no extra charge for hotel guests.

Free Wi-Fi

Fast, reliable internet throughout the hotel — in your room, the lobby, the restaurant, and the pool area.

Airport Pickup & Drop

We can arrange a car to pick you up from Tribhuvan Airport and drop you back when you leave — just let us know ahead of time.

About Us

Luxury Paradise Hotel is located in Budhanilkantha, Kathmandu — a quiet area close to the city while still giving you easy access to major temples, tourist spots, and the airport.

We have been welcoming guests since 2010. Our team works hard to make sure you have a clean, comfortable, and friendly stay — whether you are here for one night or two weeks.
